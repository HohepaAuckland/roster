================================================
  HOHEPA AUCKLAND ROSTER SYSTEM
  Local Web App — Runs on your computer
================================================

QUICK START:
  Windows:  Double-click  run.bat
  Mac/Linux: Double-click run.sh  (or open Terminal, type: bash run.sh)

Then open your browser and go to:
  http://localhost:5050

REQUIREMENTS:
  Python 3.8 or newer (free from python.org)

HOW TO USE:
  1. Upload your Excel roster file (drag & drop or click)
  2. Dashboard shows house summary + 96h alerts instantly
  3. Click "Houses" tab → pick a house → see/edit the roster grid
  4. Click any cell to edit: change name, shift, hours, leave type
  5. "Staff Summary" tab → all staff hours, colour-coded by house
  6. "Individual" tab → search any staff member → see their full schedule
  7. "Manage" tab → add new staff, edit contracts, download Excel

FEATURES:
  ✅ Upload Excel roster (Poonam format)
  ✅ 96h threshold alert (red banner + highlighted)
  ✅ House summary with hours vs budget
  ✅ Staff hours breakdown by house
  ✅ Individual roster cards (printable / email)
  ✅ Add/edit staff and shifts directly in the app
  ✅ Leave tracking (AL, SL, BL, ACC, etc.) with swap replacement
  ✅ Download updated Excel back out
  ✅ Works offline — no internet needed after install
